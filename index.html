Chenggang Wu

I'm a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the RISE Lab at U.C. Berkeley working with Professor Joe Hellerstein. My research interests lie in data-centric systems and distributed systems. In the last two years, I worked on a super-fast, flexibly consistent, autoscaling and cost-effective Key-Value Store named Anna. Currently, I am extending the core design principles of Anna and designing a programming framework for building distributed systems.

Prior to U.C. Berkeley, I obtained my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Brown University in 2015.
